Invoker Spell Guide

Cold Snap

Stuns the enemy for a short duration each time they are damaged.

Ghost Walk

Makes Invoker invisible while slowing the movement speed of nearby enemies.

Ice Wall

Generates a wall of ice that slows and damages enemies that touch it.

EMP

Detonates a mana-draining pulse at a targeted area after a delay.

Tornado

Lifts enemies into the air, disabling them and dealing damage.

Alacrity

Increases the attack speed and damage of a targeted ally or Invoker.

Sun Strike

Targets an area anywhere on the map to deal pure damage after a delay.

Forge Spirit

Summons a controllable Forge Spirit to aid Invoker in battle.

Chaos Meteor

Summons a meteor that rolls over enemies in its path, dealing damage and burning them.

Deafening Blast

Emits a powerful blast that damages enemies while disarming and knocking them back.
